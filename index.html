SUMMARY

This study is a cost-benefit analysis on clean up efforts for the Boston Harbor ecosystem. By evaluating the benefits of a cleaner environments, a monetary value estimate was produced to compare with the cost of the clean up projects. Valuation of non-market goods is one of the main challenged for this study, as economic value is a social construct. The valuation methods are described and seperated by land areas as well as the estimate valuations for each area.
